Manifesto

Built by filmmakers.
Powered by AI.

metaUNIT uses new technology on a newly built workflow — made by the people whose skills our clients already used and trusted.

01

Made by the people you trusted

We work with production designers, costume designers, DoPs and post houses. AI didn't replace them — it joined them.

02

The director is the unit

metaUNIT's core is a team working directly with the director — the closest, most direct collaboration, with communication already worked out. That's why it works.

03

Not a post house

We work with post houses. We didn't come to fight for every project — we came to lead on quality and point the direction.

04

Full-generative, full system

We work only on full-generative AI shots, and only inside a real production system — production house, post-production and the right specialists. Safety held by an AI-trained crew of experienced producers. Exactly like a normal shoot.

05

Professionals, not enthusiasts

We're not AI enthusiasts. We're professionals who expanded our skills to deliver the highest quality with the latest technology.

06

We educate first

You can't use AI effectively in the old process. Centralized decisions, communication and true partnership make quality collaboration possible — we build in-house tools we share with partners, plus pro-bono education for the industry.

who_we_are

We are not AI creators.
We are filmmakers.

We're the creators who have worked with you for years. Knowing your needs, your standards, and the hard parts of the production process, we built a workflow that gives our clients the same skills — and the same people — they have always relied on. Now powered by AI.

Filmmaking was always a director's unit. When the industry forgot that for a moment, we showed up — not to compete with post houses or other "AI creators." We offer something else. We offer what we always have: good films, campaigns that work, stories with real quality — and the same people. The only difference is tools more powerful than ever before.

We were always a unit. Today we return as metaUNIT — to lead, to educate, to create.

A directed generative pipeline. End to end.
